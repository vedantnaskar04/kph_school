School Rules and Instructions:

  • The student should be regular and punctual.
  • Students should always carry their diary with them.
  • Children accompanied by parents can't enter school before 7:25 am, and cant't remain in the school after 12:45 pm.
  • Every student should wear their ID cards at all times in the school premises.
  • Once entered in the school, he/she will not be allowed to go home during school hours.
  • Any student with symptoms of illness shall not be entertained to enter into the school.
  • Parents can meet the class teachers on Fridays during 1300 hrs.
  • Parents should ensure that the student revises whatever is taught in the school on a daily basis.
